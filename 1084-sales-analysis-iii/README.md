<p style="user-select: auto;">&nbsp;</p>

<p style="user-select: auto;">Write an SQL query that reports the <strong style="user-select: auto;">products</strong> that were <strong style="user-select: auto;">only</strong> sold in the first quarter of <code style="user-select: auto;">2019</code>. That is, between <code style="user-select: auto;">2019-01-01</code> and <code style="user-select: auto;">2019-03-31</code> inclusive.</p>

<p style="user-select: auto;">Return the result table in <strong style="user-select: auto;">any order</strong>.</p>

<p style="user-select: auto;">The query result format is in the following example.</p>

<p style="user-select: auto;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="user-select: auto;"><strong class="example" style="user-select: auto;">Example 1:</strong></p>

<pre style="user-select: auto;"><strong style="user-select: auto;">Input:</strong> 
Product table:
+------------+--------------+------------+
| product_id | product_name | unit_price |
+------------+--------------+------------+
| 1          | S8           | 1000       |
| 2          | G4           | 800        |
| 3          | iPhone       | 1400       |
+------------+--------------+------------+
Sales table:
+-----------+------------+----------+------------+----------+-------+
| seller_id | product_id | buyer_id | sale_date  | quantity | price |
+-----------+------------+----------+------------+----------+-------+
| 1         | 1          | 1        | 2019-01-21 | 2        | 2000  |
| 1         | 2          | 2        | 2019-02-17 | 1        | 800   |
| 2         | 2          | 3        | 2019-06-02 | 1        | 800   |
| 3         | 3          | 4        | 2019-05-13 | 2        | 2800  |
+-----------+------------+----------+------------+----------+-------+
<strong style="user-select: auto;">Output:</strong> 
+-------------+--------------+
| product_id  | product_name |
+-------------+--------------+
| 1           | S8           |
+-------------+--------------+
<strong style="user-select: auto;">Explanation:</strong> 
The product with id 1 was only sold in the spring of 2019.
The product with id 2 was sold in the spring of 2019 but was also sold after the spring of 2019.
The product with id 3 was sold after spring 2019.
We return only product 1 as it is the product that was only sold in the spring of 2019.
</pre>
